# 🏗️ MaterialPrice AI Predictor

### **The Intelligent Construction & Proofing Intelligence Platform**

**MaterialPrice AI Predictor** is a production-grade intelligence tool designed to help construction professionals, real estate developers, and procurement officers navigate the volatile market of building materials. 

In an industry where prices can swing overnight due to global events, this tool provides a **data-driven hedge** against market uncertainty.

---

## 🌟 What Does This Product Do?

The MaterialPrice AI Predictor provides **next-day price forecasts** for critical construction and proofing materials by merging two powerful data streams:

1.  **📈 Quantitative Market History**: Analyzes the last 90 days of high-frequency trading data, identifying "lag-based" momentum and seasonal trends.
2.  **📰 Qualitative News Sentiment**: Uses AI to scan real-time global news headlines (e.g., "cement price hike India," "Bitumen supply chain disruption") and translates them into a mathematical sentiment score.

By combining what *has* happened (history) with what people are *saying* (news), the tool produces a refined prediction model that is far more accurate than simple linear forecasting.
